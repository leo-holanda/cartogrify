1. Information Collection and Use

We collect and store limited information about you when you use Cartogrify. Specifically, we collect:

Spotify Account ID

When your Country Diversity Index (how many different countries your top artists come from) is computed, it is necessary to make sure that it is associated to you. So, when you login, it updates instead of creating a new one. That's why your Spotify Account ID is collected. It's stored in a Supabase database and it isn't share with anyone.

Country Associated with Spotify Account

Cartogrify compares your Country Diversity Index with other users from your country. For that reason, I need to collect your country data. It's stored in a Supabase database and it isn't share with anyone.
